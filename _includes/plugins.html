Neatline Plugins

NeatlineText

Connect text documents to Neatline exhibits.

NeatlineText makes it possible to connect paragraphs, sentences, and words in text documents with annotations in Neatine exhibits.

Download

NeatlineSimile

Add Simile Timeline to Neatline exhibits.

Neatline Simile adds the SIMILE Timeline widget to Neatline exhibits so that records can be plotted as points and spans on the timeline, and the timeline can be used to control the visibility of records in the exhibit.

Download

NeatlineWaypoints

Adds a list of waypoints to Neatline exhibits.

NeatlineWaypoints adds a list of clickable “waypoints” to an exhibit, making it possible to guide users through an exhibit in a specified sequence.

Download
